About BlueWater ROV Services

Family-owned and based in Holland, Michigan, BlueWater ROV Services utilizes the Chasing M2 Pro Max to offer professional visual underwater inspections that are safer, faster, and more documented than traditional diver methods.

Mission: To provide clear, actionable visual information that assists clients in understanding underwater conditions to help prevent potential issues, reduce liability concerns, and inform decisions about their investments.

Vision: To become West Michigan’s trusted provider of visual underwater inspection services with a multi-ROV fleet supporting waterfront communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your services safer than divers?
Our ROV technology eliminates human risk by keeping operators on the surface, while providing high-quality visual data.
Are your inspections certified?
No, our services provide visual inspections for informational purposes only and do not constitute certified engineering or structural assessments. We recommend consulting a licensed engineer for official certifications.
Can you operate in winter?
Yes, we specialize in year-round services, including safe under-ice operations using specialized protocols.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve Allegan, Ottawa, Muskegon, and Oceana Counties in West Michigan, with potential expansion to adjacent areas.

Why BlueWater ROV?

A professional, ROV-first inspection company — not a dive shop and not a hobby drone.

Our mission is to deliver clear, actionable underwater information that helps you prevent damage, reduce liability, and protect your investment. We operate under a simple philosophy: safety first, clarity & transparency, reliability, professionalism, and innovation.

Commercial-Grade ROV Platform

We deploy a Chasing M2 Pro Max with 4K camera, high-lumen lighting, and extended tether, allowing precise, stable inspections of docks, lifts, seawalls, marinas, and swim areas without putting anyone in the water.

Year-Round & Under-Ice Capability

Unlike divers, we operate in the seasons when risk is highest — after storms, during freeze-thaw cycles, and under solid ice using specialized winter and under-ice SOPs for safe, controlled launches and retrievals.

4K Documentation & Clear Reports

You receive a download link to your 4K footage, annotated photos, and a structured findings summary that’s easy to share with contractors, boards, buyers, and carriers — aligned with your inspection and report templates.

ROV vs. Diver Advantage

ROV inspections deliver safer, lower-cost, better-documented results for most situations. Divers still matter when physical touch is required — but for 90% of inspection needs, ROV wins on risk, cost, and speed.

  • • Zero human exposure vs. diver risk in cold, dark, or icy water.
  • • Flat-rate pricing vs. $250–$450/hr diver billing.
  • • Stable 4K video and photos vs. limited or no documentation.
  • • Full under-ice operation vs. no diver access in those conditions.

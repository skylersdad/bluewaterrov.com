Why BlueWater ROV?

We combine specialized equipment, local experience, and clear communication so you always know what’s happening below the surface — and what to do next.

Advanced ROV Technology

Our Chasing M2 Pro Max ROV captures stable 4K video with powerful lighting and maneuverability, allowing us to inspect areas divers often can’t safely reach.

True Under-Ice Capability

Winter in West Michigan is when many problems reveal themselves. We operate safely under ice, keeping you out of the water while still getting the full picture.

Clear, Actionable Reports

You’ll receive a private link to your 4K footage, still photos, and a plain-language findings summary that’s easy to share with contractors, buyers, or carriers.

Local, Owner-Operated

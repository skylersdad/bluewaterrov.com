Why BlueWater ROV Services?

Not a dive shop. Not a hobby drone. A dedicated, owner-operated ROV inspection service built for West Michigan lakes.

When you hire BlueWater, you’re working directly with the owner-operator who pilots the ROV, reviews the footage, and writes your report. That means less confusion, faster answers, and a single point of accountability from first call through final deliverables.

Purpose-Built ROV Platform

We use a commercial-grade ROV with 4K camera, high-output lighting, stabilizing thrusters, and a long tether. The system is tuned specifically for inland lakes and West Michigan water conditions.

  • Stable footage even in low-visibility conditions.
  • Ability to work in tight spaces around lifts, pilings, and seawalls.
  • Live control on site so we can linger on areas of concern.

Safe, Year-Round Operation

Winter and early-season inspections are when you learn the most about stress, ice movement, and damage. Our under-ice SOP keeps everyone out of the water while the ROV does the work.

  • Under-ice and cold-water capability with controlled access points.
  • No diver risk, no exposure to cold shock or entanglement.
  • Ideal after storms, freeze-thaw cycles, or suspected ice shove damage.

Actionable Reporting, Not Just Video

You don’t have time to scrub through an hour of raw footage. We provide clear callouts and easy-to-share summaries designed for homeowners, boards, contractors, and adjusters.

  • Download link to organized 4K video and key photos.
  • Condition scoring and “Monitor / Plan Repair / Act Now” style recommendations.
  • Language you can drop directly into board packets and claim files.

Local, Owner-Operated & Aligned With You

BlueWater is based in Holland and focused on the West Michigan lakeshore. We understand local lake levels, bottom conditions, and the contractors you’re likely to work with.

  • Direct communication with the person on the controls.
  • Flexible scheduling for storms, closings, and urgent concerns.
  • Transparent pricing and no surprise add-ons on site.

ROV vs. Diver: The Smart First Step

Divers still have a place when a repair requires hands-on work. But as a first look, ROV inspections usually give you more information with less risk and cost.

  • • Zero human exposure vs. diver risk in cold, dark, or icy water.
  • • Flat-rate pricing vs. hourly diver billing.
  • • High-quality documentation vs. “you had to be there.”
  • • Easy to share with multiple decision-makers at once.

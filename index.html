Why Choose BlueWater ROV Services?

Purpose-built ROV inspections — not divers, not hobby drones. Just clean documentation, safe operation, and actionable results.

Commercial-Grade ROV Platform

Our ROV system provides highly stable 4K video, powerful adjustable lighting, precision maneuvering around tight lakefront structures, and a long tether allowing flexible access in challenging or murky water.

  • Stabilized video for low-visibility conditions
  • High-output lighting for dark or deep water
  • Ideal for docks, lifts, pilings, seawalls & debris surveys

Safe, Year-Round Operation

No divers, no risk, no exposure. Winter and under-ice inspections are performed using a clean access hole, controlled tethering, continuous monitoring, and an abort-ready safety protocol.

  • Under-ice capability with controlled launch procedure
  • No diver fatigue, entanglement, or cold-shock hazards
  • Ideal after storms or freeze-thaw cycles

Actionable Reporting

Every client receives organized video files, key still photos, and a plain-language findings summary structured for homeowners, HOAs, contractors, adjusters, or real-estate agents.

  • Download link to 4K video + documentation
  • Clear recommendations: Monitor / Plan Repair / Act Now
  • Ready for board packets, bids & insurance files

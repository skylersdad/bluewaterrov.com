Our Services

Serving Allegan, Ottawa, Muskegon, and Oceana Counties with safer, more affordable inspections than traditional divers. Year-round operations, including under-ice capabilities.

Dock & Boat Lift Inspections

4K video scans of cables, pulleys, cradles, anchors, and structural integrity. Basic to full assessments.

Seawall & Erosion Assessments

Vertical passes identifying cracks, undermining, toe scour, and ice damage for shoreline protection.

HOA Swim-Area Safety Scans

Grid-pattern sweeps for hazards like rocks, debris, and drop-offs, with depth mapping.

Marina Slip-by-Slip Surveys

Piling checks, anchor reviews, and hazard mapping for full facility compliance and safety.

Under-Ice Winter Inspections

Safe operations through heated mats and protocols to document ice pressure damage.

Insurance & Storm Damage Documentation

Time-stamped evidence for claims, with condition scoring and annotated reports.

About BlueWater ROV Services

Family-owned and based in Holland, Michigan, BlueWater ROV Services uses the Chasing M2 Pro Max to provide professional underwater inspections that are safer, faster, and more documented than diver services.

Mission: To deliver clear, actionable underwater information that helps clients prevent damage, reduce liability, and protect their investments.

Vision: To become West Michigan’s leading provider of underwater inspection services with a multi-ROV fleet serving waterfront communities.
